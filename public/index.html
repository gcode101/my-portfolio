About

Hello there! I'm Guelmis Cortina, a US Armed Forces veteran and Software Engineer with a bachelor’s degree in Computer Science. Currently, I call the vibrant city of Miami, Florida, my home.

My journey in the world of technology began during my time in the military, where I developed a deep appreciation for precision, discipline, and problem-solving. After transitioning to civilian life, I pursued my passion for coding and earned a degree in Computer Science.

I bring proficiency in Java, SQL, PHP, Javascript, React, Node JS, Express JS, and MongoDB, coupled with hands-on experience collaborating with cross-disciplinary teams in an agile environment. My focus lies in designing and developing cutting-edge products and services that make a meaningful impact. With a strong foundation in Computer Science fundamentals, including object-oriented design, algorithm design, data structures, problem-solving, and complex analysis, I am driven to overcome challenges and create innovative solutions.
